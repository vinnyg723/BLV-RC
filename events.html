Events

As the BRC continues to grow, we continue to become involved with additional events.

What else do we do besides running?

If you are looking for information concerning local races, please look at the Run Belleville section of our site.

Belleville Chili Cookoff

Chili? What the heck does chili have to do with running. Nothing. But the Belleville Chili Cookoff is a fun event, it’s currently our major fundraiser, and it’s a great way to get our name out to new people.

River to River Relay

River to River Relay is held in April of each year and the BRC has entered teams for a decade. (read more)

Adopt a Trail

Don’t forget to pick up any litter you see along the trail and keep an eye out for anything that needs to be taken care of (potholes, lights out, etc). Send an e-mail to Us and we’ll forward the info to Parks and Recreation.
