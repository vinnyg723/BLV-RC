A group of runners in & around Belleville, IL

Welcome to the website for the Belleville Running Club. Consider this your portal to the world of the BRC. We’re a group of runners in and around Belleville, IL.

Runner Friendly Community

Belleville, Illinois has been named an RRCA Runner Friendly Community for 2014-2019.

Belleville Main Street Marathon

The Belleville Running Club and Toolen’s Running Start conducted the Third Annual Belleville Main Street Marathon on September 29, 2018 and it was a resounding success! Mark your calendars for the 4th Annual Belleville Main Street Marathon, which will take place Saturday, Sept. 28, 2019.
